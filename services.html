Our Services

We offer a range of services to help you access information, build research skills, and make the most of our medical resources.

Library Services

Circulation Services

Borrow, renew, or return books and other materials with the help of our circulation desk staff.

Reference & Research Help

Get assistance in locating books, journals, and trustworthy medical resources for your studies or research.

Online Resources

Access our growing collection of e-books, journals, and medical databases—available both on campus and remotely.

Research Support

Receive one-on-one help with research strategies, literature searches, and citation management tools.

Information Literacy Sessions

Join our training sessions to learn how to find, evaluate, and properly cite reliable medical information.

Printing and Scanning

Use our printing and scanning facilities for your study and research needs, available during library hours.
