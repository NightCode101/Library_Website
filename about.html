About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to medical education.

Our Story

Aurora's Medical Library was established to support the learning, teaching, and research needs of students, educators, and healthcare professionals. Our mission is to provide access to trusted medical information and to foster a culture of continuous discovery.

Our Mission

To provide reliable health information, support teaching and research, and ensure that students, faculty, and healthcare professionals have access to the resources they need to succeed and make informed decisions.

Vision

To be the leading source of medical information and learning, supporting excellence in education, research, and patient care for our academic community.
