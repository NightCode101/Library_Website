Programs & Initiatives

Learn more about how Aurora’s Medical Library supports education, research, and community health.

Our Ongoing Programs

Health Literacy Campaign

We visit local communities and schools to share practical health information and promote informed decision-making.

Research Mentorship

Librarians and faculty members guide students through the process of developing and publishing research projects.

Digital Library Access

Students and researchers can use our online journals, e-books, and databases anytime to support their studies and clinical work.

Wellness Week

In partnership with local health units, we host free seminars, check-ups, and discussions on preventive healthcare.

Library Orientation & Skills Training

We help new users learn how to find reliable information, cite sources correctly, and make the most of digital research tools.
